HAPPY HABSBURGS

A two piece bedroom band from Perth in Australia

We, Happy Habsburgs, are a band from Perth Western Australia. We're just two boys. We write songs that mean something to us and hope they mean something to you.

We write and record simple and intimate songs in our homes. We'd like you to listen to them with your headphones on, late at night in your bedrooms.

Happy Habsburgs is Jimmy Hillis and Tyler Kelly.

Our first recordings are available for free online at Soundcloud. Collectively, we're calling it Friends, Sex, Lovers. It's made up of three songs, each titled to match the collective title by it's track position. The titles, and songs themselves, describe the process by which we believe relationships are planted, grown and survive.
